Methods of teaching English - programs:

At Headway Institute we use the most up-to-date English teaching methods and provide high quality English lessons. Arabic Language Courses

Method

At Headway Institute (Arabic language centre in Dubai), teaching begins with the spoken language where emphasis is on the communicative properties of learning a language. Our language techniques are designed to engage in the pragmatic, authentic and functional use of the language in an interactive and stimulating way. Learning is facilitated in an environment that is as comfortable as possible, and our highly qualified and experienced teachers guarantee learning with comprehensible input.
